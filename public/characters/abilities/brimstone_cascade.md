## Brimstone Cascade
_Planar fury_

**Rank:** I  
**Cost:** Corruption, 1d4  
**Range:** 4'  
**Known By**: Sorcerers

The mystic lashes out, hurling an arc of fire and brimstone toward the target, dealing 1d12 fire damage.  
A save vs Blast reduces this by half.
